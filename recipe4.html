Chowmein

Chowmein is a popular noodle dish made by stir-frying noodles with vegetables and sauces. It is quick, flavorful, and widely loved.

25 mins Easy

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Capsicum
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil noodles and drain
  2. Heat oil and sauté garlic
  3. Add vegetables and stir fry
  4. Add noodles and sauces
  5. Mix well and serve hot
