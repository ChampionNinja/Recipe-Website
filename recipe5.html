Samosa

Samosa is a crispy fried snack filled with spiced potatoes and peas. It is a classic street food known for its crunchy texture and rich flavor.

50 mins Medium

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Green peas
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare dough using flour
  2. Make spiced potato filling
  3. Shape samosas
  4. Deep fry until golden brown
  5. Serve hot
