Faluda

Faluda is a layered dessert drink combining rose syrup, milk, vermicelli and ice cream. It is refreshing, visually appealing, and perfect as a sweet treat.

20 mins Easy

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Rose syrup
  • Vermicelli
  • Sabja seeds
  • Ice cream

Steps

  1. Soak sabja seeds
  2. Boil vermicelli
  3. Mix syrup with milk
  4. Layer ingredients
  5. Serve chilled
