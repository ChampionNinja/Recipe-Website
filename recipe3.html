Pina Colada

Pina Colada is a refreshing tropical drink made with pineapple and coconut. It is smooth, sweet, and perfect for a cooling beverage.

10 mins Easy

Ingredients

  • Pineapple juice
  • Coconut milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour into a glass
  4. Serve chilled
